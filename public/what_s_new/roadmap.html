Roadmap

Updated: December 12th 2017

We at Spryker are happy to share our plans with you. Our plans are guidelines that give us direction, that move us forward to continuously evolve and improve our product. However, we are also flexible and we constantly listen and adapt. Therefore, our plans tend to change. So although we are good fulfilling our commitments, we reserve the right to change our priorities, remove or add new features from time to time. If you are planning anything strategic based on this list, you might want to talk to us first, either by contacting your Spryker representative or one of our Solution Partners. You are also welcome to contribute to the roadmap discussion in our Forum: Feature Roadmap Group.

If you see a feature that you like, send us a mail and let us know why the feature is important to you.

We have 2 categories in our roadmap:

Upcoming Releases which are in the short-term pipeline and have a somewhat high chance to be released in the upcoming months.

Future Releases are high-level raw backlog items that we plan to develop. You can easily contribute to these features by sharing with us how a given feature from this list is beneficial for your business, share your priorities or simply tell us what might be missing for you. Your input and needs are ideal to help shape upcoming feature releases. Please keep in mind, that certain things will not be built into core if we consider them to be too specific to a certain business case. However, the more you contribute the more you will help us know what you need to grow with us.

The roadmap contains features not architectural items, enhancements, technology updates or any other strategic releases we are working on. We kindly ask you not to base any business decision on these lists without consulting with us first.

Items are listed in no particular order. The order has no relevance to when they will be developed or released:

Upcoming Releases

  • Calculating product availability per store
  • Assigning product abstracts to specific stores
  • Full enablement of multi-currency concept, gradually enabling every relevant functionality with multi-currency
  • Managing filters per category, filter whitelist per category
  • Switching between list and grid view in the catalog
  • Changing the number of products displayed on every page of the catalog

Future Releases

  • Store-specific discounts
  • Full support for multi-store and multi-currency concepts
  • Setting store-specific product prices
  • Store-specific product search
  • Sharing stock and reservations between all shop installations
  • Store-specific cart
  • Store-specific CMS pages
  • Microdata for product details page
  • Full support for multi-store and multi-currency concepts
  • Store-specific shipments
  • Picture upload via admin backend
  • Ordering carousel items for homepage from admin backend
  • Google Tag Manager integration
  • Google Analytics integration
  • Adding multiple products to the cart at once

B2B Features

  • Orders parking
  • Search restrictions per customer account and/or role
  • Company account with mini ACL
  • Measuring units for products
  • Persisted shopping cart
  • Multiple shopping lists
  • Full support of "business on behalf" concept
  • Request for Quote functionality
  • Language switcher without redirect
  • Handling returns from customer account
  • Demo user account in shop
  • Customer specific prices
  • Hiding price and stock for not logged in users
  • Quick add to cart functionality with article number and quantity
  • Comment for order
  • Comparing products from the same category
  • Generating proposals of products
  • Importing products to cart from a .csv file
  • CMS pages in search results
  • Ariba integration
  • OCI integration
  • Determining pricing scale based on product quantity
